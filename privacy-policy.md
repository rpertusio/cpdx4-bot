Cpdx4‑Bot — Privacy Policy
Last Updated: January 2026
1. Data Collection
Cpdx4‑Bot does not store any personal data.
The Bot only processes information required to:
- Receive commands from Discord
- Report Natro Macro status
- Execute remote control actions
No logs, messages, IDs, or user data are stored outside of Discord’s systems.
2. Temporary Processing
The Bot may temporarily process:
- Command content
- User IDs (only as part of Discord’s built‑in message metadata)
- Macro status information
This data is not saved, not shared, and not used for analytics.
3. Third‑Party Services
The Bot interacts only with:
- Discord (for commands and responses)
- Natro Macro (for status and control)
No additional third‑party services are used.
4. Children’s Privacy
The Bot does not target or collect data from children.
Use of the Bot by minors must comply with Discord’s age requirements.
5. Changes to This Policy
This Privacy Policy may be updated at any time. Continued use of the Bot after changes are posted constitutes acceptance of the updated Policy.
